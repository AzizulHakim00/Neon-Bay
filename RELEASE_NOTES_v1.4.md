# Neon Bay v1.4 — Vice Coast

Vice Coast is the largest Neon Bay upgrade so far. It adds a second five-mission chapter, a stronger 1980s tropical identity, substantial shader and lighting improvements, a nightclub, harbor and downtown expansion, procedural radio stations, business ownership, a phone/map interface, an SMG and additional vehicle classes.

## Key changes

- Ten total story missions
- Animated ocean and sky shaders
- Bloom and enhanced neon lighting
- Five city districts
- Five playable interiors
- Fourteen route traffic vehicles
- Three purchasable businesses
- Three original radio stations
- VICE SMG
- Save schema v4

## Important legal note

This is an original game inspired by the general tropical open-world crime genre. No GTA: Vice City map, character, logo, music, dialogue, mission script or proprietary art is included.
