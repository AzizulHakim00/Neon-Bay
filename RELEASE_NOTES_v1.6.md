# Neon Bay v1.6 — Cinematic City Overhaul

Version 1.6 rebuilds the visual presentation around richer materials, grounded lighting, living architecture and scalable cinematic post-processing.

## City and material upgrades

- Procedural road surface with cracks, wear, oil stains and wet-response shading
- Crosswalks, manholes, curb drains and persistent skid marks
- Procedural façade detail and lightweight bump mapping
- Living hotel, office and residential windows with fake room interiors and time-based lighting
- Wet shoreline sand, footprints and animated sunlight/moonlight glitter across the coast
- Denser modular street detail and parking meters

## Lighting and post-processing

- Time-driven moving directional sunlight
- Quality-scaled soft shadow maps with improved bias and distance control
- Character and vehicle contact shadows
- SSAO on High and Ultra
- FXAA plus cinematic color grading, vignette, grain, damage chromatic shift and cutscene focus treatment
- District and mission-sensitive visual grading

## Vehicles and characters

- Reverse lights, turn indicators and dashboard glow
- Exhaust smoke that responds to engine health
- Tire smoke/skid decals during hard braking and cornering
- Improved actor material response and role-sensitive contact shadows

## Weather and atmosphere

- Gradual road wetness instead of instant material switching
- Moving cloud banks with clear, overcast and rain coloration
- Lightning flashes during storms
- Rain ripples around the player
- Weather-responsive shoreline and reflective surfaces

## Presentation

- Animated chapter/job title cards for all ten missions
- Stronger mission-specific cinematic grade
- Quality scaling across Low, Medium, High and Ultra

## Compatibility

- Preserves save schema v4
- Preserves all ten story missions, businesses, radio, phone, interiors and side activities
