# Neon Bay v1.1 Release Notes

Neon Bay v1.1 upgrades the Chapter One foundation without expanding into an unmanageable open world. The release concentrates on visible polish and stronger gameplay feedback.

## Highlights

- Animated articulated player
- Three unique vehicle designs and handling profiles
- Shotgun and weapon switching
- Tactical enemy search and cover behavior
- Ocean Drive environmental redesign
- First Ride cinematic and dialogue
- Modular architecture
- Browser-based animated trailer and six vector release screenshots

## Compatibility

- Desktop Chrome, Edge and Firefox are the primary targets.
- Touch controls are included for capable mobile browsers.
- A WebGL-capable browser and hardware acceleration are recommended.
- Existing version 1 saves migrate to the new version 2 weapon-aware save structure.
