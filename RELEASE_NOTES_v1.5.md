# Neon Bay v1.5 — Graphics & World Overhaul

Version 1.5 focuses on visual quality, feedback and city density while preserving all ten missions, businesses, radio stations, activities and version 4 save data.

## Graphics improvements

- Ultra graphics preset
- Clearcoat vehicle paint and improved glass materials
- Dynamic player-vehicle headlights at night
- Rain-responsive reflective road puddles
- Neon light pools and stronger nighttime atmosphere
- Animated beach foam and coastal haze
- Atmospheric dust and illuminated particles
- Additional street props and environmental detail
- Adaptive cinematic exposure and weather fog
- Bullet tracers, muzzle flashes and impact sparks
- Recoil and collision camera shake
- Film-grain and vignette presentation pass

## Performance

All effects are quality-scaled. Low mode disables the most expensive atmosphere and lighting effects, while Medium, High and Ultra progressively increase visual density.
